Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa process take?

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Typically:

  • Student Visas: 4-8 weeks
  • Work Visas: 8-16 weeks
  • PR Applications: 6-12 months

We provide realistic timelines during consultation.

What documents do I need?

Basic requirements include:

  • Passport (valid for 6+ months)
  • Educational certificates
  • Work experience proofs
  • Financial documents
  • Medical certificates

We provide a customized checklist based on your profile.
