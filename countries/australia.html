Skilled migration and study opportunities with excellent post-study work rights, beautiful landscapes, and high standard of living.

Australia Immigration Programs

Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189)

Points-based visa for skilled workers who are not sponsored by an employer, state or territory, or family member. This visa allows you to live and work in Australia permanently.

Processing Time: 8-12 months

Points Required: 65+

IELTS Requirement: 6.0+ bands each

Visa Type: Permanent Resident

Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Study in Australian universities with post-study work rights and pathway to permanent residency. Australia offers world-class education and research opportunities.

Processing Time: 4-8 weeks

IELTS Requirement: 6.0+ bands

Post-Study Work: 2-4 years

Work Rights: 40 hours/fortnight

Employer Sponsored Visa

Work for an approved employer in Australia with pathway to permanent residency. Various subclasses available based on occupation and employer sponsorship.

Processing Time: 6-12 months

Job Offer: Required

Sponsorship: Employer must be approved

PR Pathway: After 3 years
