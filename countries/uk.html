World-class education and career opportunities with various visa pathways in one of the world's most prestigious destinations.

UK Immigration Programs

Skilled Worker Visa

Work in the UK with a licensed sponsor. Points-based system with minimum salary requirements.

Processing Time: 3-8 weeks

IELTS Requirement: B1 level

Minimum Salary: £26,200+

Student Visa (Tier 4)

Study at UK universities with post-study work visa (Graduate Route) for 2-3 years.

Processing Time: 3-6 weeks

IELTS Requirement: 5.5+ bands

Post-Study Work: 2-3 years

Global Talent Visa

For leaders and potential leaders in academia, research, arts, culture, and digital technology.

Processing Time: 4-8 weeks

Endorsement: Required

Fields: Academia, Arts, Tech
